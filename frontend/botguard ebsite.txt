## **How It Works**

1. Visitor request enters your site.
2. BotGuard Pro inspects headers, IP reputation, and behavior.
3. Good traffic passes instantly; bad traffic is blocked or throttled.
4. Logs feed into your dashboard and global threat-intel pool.
